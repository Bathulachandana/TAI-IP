        <section class="instructions">
            <h2>INSTRUCTIONS:</h2>
            <ol>
                <li>Wash and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes.</li>
                <li>In a large pan, heat ghee or oil and fry the onions until golden brown. Remove half of the fried onions and set aside for garnishing.</li>
                <li>Add garlic, ginger, and tomatoes to the remaining onions and cook until tomatoes are soft.</li>
                <li>Add chicken pieces, yogurt, and all the spices. Cook until chicken is almost done.</li>
                <li>Drain the soaked rice and add it to the pan. Stir well.</li>
                <li>Add water and bring to a boil. Then, reduce heat, cover, and cook until rice and chicken are fully cooked.</li>
                <li>Garnish with fried onions and chopped coriander leaves. Serve hot.</li>
            </ol>
